# Superstore Sales — Exploratory Data Analysis

A business-style exploratory data analysis (EDA) on the Superstore Sales dataset, uncovering key insights about sales performance across categories, regions, customer segments, and shipping behavior.

## 📊 Executive Summary

This analysis reveals a business driven primarily by the Consumer segment (over half of revenue) and the Technology category, with Copiers and Machines as top sub-categories. Sales are concentrated in key markets — led by Florida and Jacksonville — with the South region showing the highest volume but also the most volatility. Customers favor Standard Class shipping despite Same Day delivery yielding higher average sales, pointing to a possible opportunity to promote faster options. Sales peak consistently every September and November, useful for inventory and marketing planning.

## 🔍 Key Findings

- **Top Category:** Technology leads in average sales per order
- **Top Sub-Category:** Copiers, followed by Machines
- **Top Region:** South has the highest sales volume
- **Top State & City:** Florida and Jacksonville
- **Customer Segments:** Consumer (50.8%), Corporate (30.4%), Home Office (18.8%)
- **Shipping:** Standard Class is most used; Same Day shows highest average order value
- **Seasonality:** Sales peak every September and November, dip in February and October
